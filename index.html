About Sports Authority of Goa

Promoting Excellence in Sports Across the State of Goa

The Sports Authority of Goa (SAG) is committed to the development, promotion, and regulation of sports activities across the state. Through structured training programs, infrastructure development, and talent identification initiatives, SAG works towards nurturing athletes and strengthening Goa’s sporting culture at grassroots, district, state, and national levels.

Traditional Sports Promotion

Goa has a rich heritage of traditional sports such as Kabaddi, Kho-Kho, Wrestling, Logorio, and various indigenous rural games. The Authority actively supports and promotes these disciplines through competitions, awareness programs, and school-level participation drives.

Athlete Development & Support

The Authority provides coaching facilities, financial assistance schemes, scholarships, and access to modern training infrastructure to support athletes in achieving excellence at national and international platforms.

  • • Development and maintenance of sports complexes and stadiums across Goa
  • • Organization of inter-school, district, and state-level tournaments
  • • Hosting of national championships and sporting events
  • • Grassroots talent identification and youth training programs
  • • Support for women in sports and inclusive participation initiatives

Traditional Sports of Goa

Preserving and Promoting Goa’s Indigenous Sporting Heritage

Lagori (Seven Stones)

Lagori is one of Goa’s most popular traditional team games, where players aim to knock down a stack of seven stones and rebuild it while the opposing team attempts to eliminate them using a ball.

Read More

Atya Patya

Atya Patya is a fast-paced indigenous sport that requires agility, speed, and strategic movement.

Read More

Kho-Kho & Kabaddi

Kho-Kho and Kabaddi are among the most actively promoted traditional sports in Goa. These team-based games focus on stamina, tactical planning, and teamwork, and are regularly included in district and state-level tournaments.

Read More

Langadi, Ringaani & GodDyaani

Langadi, Ringaani, and GodDyaani reflect Goa’s rich rural sporting traditions. These community-based games foster balance, coordination, and social bonding among children and youth.

Read More

The Sports Authority of Goa continues to integrate traditional sports into structured development programs, ensuring that cultural heritage and modern sporting excellence progress together.
