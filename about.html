Sports Authority of Goa

Excellence in Sports through Grassroot Development

Introduction

The Sports Authority of Goa is an Autonomous Body registered under Societies Registration Act 1860 on 5th February 1988. It promotes Sports & Games in the State and maintains major stadiums and complexes on behalf of the Ministry of Sports & Youth Affairs.

SAG ensures development at State, National & International levels by providing infrastructure, technical knowledge and equipment at grassroot level to build a Healthy & Fit Goa.

Functions & Duties

  • Plan, develop and maintain sports infrastructure in Goa.
  • Provide technical knowledge & equipment at grassroot level.
  • Register & release grants to Sports Associations & Clubs.
  • Cash awards to medal winning sportspersons.
  • Issue Form II & III for employment and merit marks.
  • Conduct competitions under Khelo India Scheme.
  • Scientific coaching in various disciplines.
  • Tribal sports competitions & scholarships.
  • Financial assistance for training & participation.
  • Encouraging rural sports talent.

Vision For The Future

“Excellence in Sports at State, National and International Level, through grass root Sports activities by motivating and encouraging the Sportspersons in achieving their GOALS”.
