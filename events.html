Goa State Marathon 2026

Date: 10 March 2026

Venue: Panaji City

Categories: 5K | 10K | Half Marathon

The Goa State Marathon 2026 is organized by the Sports Authority of Goa to promote endurance sports and fitness culture across the state. The event encourages participation from youth, senior citizens, professional athletes, and fitness enthusiasts.

  • Official timing chips provided
  • Medical & hydration support
  • Cash prizes for top finishers
  • Participation certificates for all
Registration Open
Khelo India State Championship 2026

Date: 5 April 2026

Venue: Fatorda Stadium, Margao

Sports Included: Athletics, Football, Kabaddi, Volleyball

Conducted under the Khelo India initiative, this championship identifies and nurtures young sporting talent across Goa. District-level winners compete at the state level for qualification into national competitions.

  • Taluka & District qualifiers
  • Professional referees & officials
  • Government-certified participation
  • Scholarship eligibility for winners
Entries Open
All Goa Football League 2026

Date: 1 May 2026

Venue: Mapusa & Fatorda Grounds

Category: Senior & U-19 Teams

The All Goa Football League is one of the most anticipated football competitions in the state. Organized in collaboration with Goa Football Association, the league promotes grassroots and professional football.

  • Club-based competition
  • Scouting for national selection
  • Live match updates
  • Trophy & cash rewards
